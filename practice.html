My coding journey

My coding journey began in early 2016 when I was first introducted to Launch Code & I began studying in the CS50 program. I had no idea what I was in for, but I was happy to be there! I made friends, had study partners and things were going well even though this course was VERY difficult! We were all there to learn, well, most of of us anyway.

Things were going well, I was featured in an article in EQ Magazine and got to learn CS50 directly from the people who teach it as the CS50 Hackathon!

Unfortunately, I stopped coding completely and was no longer learning. I felt with tons of dissappointment because I couldn't follow through as my classmates did. After moving beyond all of that, I am NOW thrilled to be in Code Camp 2019 & I couldn't be happier about it!
